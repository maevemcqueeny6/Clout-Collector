Clout Collector

Clout is defined as influence or power in political and community circles. Originally popularized by Chicago journalist in the Sixties and early Seventies, but since appropriated by Hollywood and influencers, including the Kardashian clan.

Kris Jenner, matriarch of the Kardashains, is the Queen of Clout, and its manager. Kylie Jenners big pregnancy reveal led to a rise in revenue for the clan and gave rise to the popular phrase, "The Devil works hard but Kris Jenner works harder."

Thus sets the stage for the Kardashian Clout Collector Game.

There are four clickable clout objects below, whose value is untold. Check the Clout Calculator as you grow closer trying to achieving that fickle friend named Fame.
However, beware: overshoot Fame and land yourself Infamy, resulting in a loss.

Each time the game starts, the Clout Calculator will change values of each object and the aspirational number needed to achieve Fame.
