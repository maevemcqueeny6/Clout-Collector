Clout Collector

Clout is defined as influence or power in political and community circles. Originally popularized by Chicago journalist Mike Royko to describe local movers and shakers when Richard J. Daley was mayor in the Sixties and early Seventies, but since appropriated by Hollywood and influencers, including the Kardashian clan.

Kris Jenner, matriarch of the Kardashains and literal progenitor of fame and fortune, is the Queen of Clout. Kylie Jenners big pregnancy reveal led to a rise in revenue for the clan and gave rise to the popular phrase, "The Devil works hard but Kris Jenner works harder."

Thus sets the stage for the Kardashian Clout Collector Game.

Fame is fickle, what gains notoriety one day is devalued the next (like work ethic, or fit tea). As such, you will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four cloutable stimuli below. By clicking on an image you will add a specific (but unbeknownst to you) amount of points to your total core.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number, because there's a thin line between fame and notoriety.

The value of each image is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change values of each object with its "cloutable" value to fame and fortune.
